VI International Conference on Mathematical Foundations of Informatics 2020

MFOI 2019 will be organized by the Faculty of Computer Science and Cybernetics within Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv, the third oldest and the biggest university in Ukraine, established in 1834. The univeristy consists of thirteen faculties and eight institutes with over 30000 students studying here and is often ranked as the best university in Ukraine.

Both the main conference track and the workshops will be hosted in the Faculty of Computer Science and Cybernetics building, located within 10 minutes walk from Vystavkovyi Tsentr metropoliten station.

Travel

Kyiv can be reached via two international airports, Igor Sikorsky Kyiv International Airport, located about 7km southwest of the city center, and Boryspil International Airport, located in Boryspil 29km east of Kyiv. Both airports service direct flights to various international destinations on every day basis. Further information about flights can be found on the airports respective websites.

The typical way to reach your destination within Kyiv from the airports is by taxi. The ride may take from 15 minutes to an hour, depending on airport and destination. The average rate charged by the taxis is about 0.40-0.50 Euros (7-10 UAH) per kilometer with a minimum of 35-40 UAH. In the historic center of the city, the rates tend to be more expensive due to traffic and tend to be around 1.5-2.5 Euros (35-60 UAH) per kilometer.
As alternative, there is a railway connection to the Kyiv-Passazhirsky Railway Station for both airports, as well as various bus and minibus lines connecting them to Kyiv Metro stations.

Visa Information

Entry regime to Ukraine for citizens of different countries can be checked here. Detailed information including the process to obtain a visa can be found on the site of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Ukraine. Normally, the visa that would be required for attending a conference is a short-term (C-type) visa. Note that there exists an option of applying for an electronic visa online.
